## 1. Core Monetization Model: Open Core + Managed Cloud

ZELOCORECMS uses a **pure "Open Core" model** with a critical difference: **the core has ALL features**. We only monetize hosting, support, and managed services — never features.


## 6. Pricing Strategy Rationale

### Why these price points?
- **Starter at $19:** Under the "credit card limit" — can be expensed without approval. Lower than Strapi Cloud, lower than Sanity.
- **Pro at $79:** Under $100 psychological barrier. Comparable to Ghost Pro, Contentful Team.
- **Business at $299:** Significantly cheaper than Strapi Team ($499/month), Contentful Scale ($880+/month), Sanity Business ($949+/month).
- **Enterprise:** Custom — negotiate based on size, usage, compliance needs.

### Annual Discount Rationale (2 months free)
Standard in SaaS. Improves cash flow, reduces churn, increases commitment.

## 7. Anti-Goals (What We Will NEVER Do)

1. ❌ **Never gate core CMS features** — no "Pro only" for basic functionality
2. ❌ **Never add transaction fees** — unlike Shopify's 0.5–2% fees
3. ❌ **Never remove features from self-hosted version** — the product must be useful without Cloud
4. ❌ **Never sell user data** — privacy is non-negotiable
5. ❌ **Never acquire and shut down** competitor products (GPL-style kill tactics)
6. ❌ **Never do VC-funded "growth at all costs"** without revenue viability
